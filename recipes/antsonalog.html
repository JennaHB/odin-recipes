Ants On a Log

Description

This is a fun snack that kids can make. It consists of artfully arranged celery, peanut butter, and raisins.

Ingredients

  • 5 stalks celery
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ¼ cup raisins

Steps

  1. Cut the celery stalks in half. Spread with peanut butter. Sprinkle with raisins.
